Bigdate Research and UX Design for Nation Center for Performaning Arts

Client / Beijing Philharmonic Association, Concept Project
Time / Mid 2017
MyRole / Direct Designer


Brief

Beijing Philhamonic Association, in which are classic musicians and music fans. The purpose of the association is to communicate and review concerts, operas and dramas. In the daily I found some opportunities to enhance the experience from booking to backing home. Then I decided to do this concept project to find out the realities and new possibilities of what the experience will be.
