Prototype

The home page is presented in the form of a card, with smart recommendations as the main body, allowing the user to directly obtain the required information at the corresponding time. When the user is listening to a concert, the card of the concert is topped, and after clicking, the information of the concert is presented in a dark mode, avoiding interference with other people by using the mobile phone to illuminate the show folding. At the same time, because the network signal is shielded during performance of the concert hall, the user does not browse other information. In addition, smart recommendations, friend dynamics, and operational pushes are presented in the form of cards.

In the performance, the top position is the operating position, including smart recommendation and advertising. Performances are categorized by venue and time, and users can quickly find the desired performance. In the show introduction interface, there are recommendations from experienced people to help novices choose to perform. The ticket purchase process can be completed in one page, with the choice of screenings, seat selection and payment completed.

Talks is an interactive community for fans to increase user interaction and stickiness.

On the Profile page, you can quickly find bookings and collections.
